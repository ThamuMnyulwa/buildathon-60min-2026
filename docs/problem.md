# AI for Health

## The Context

As of 2026, a child with acute malaria, pneumonia, dehydration, or severe malnutrition in a rural district can deteriorate within hours if treatment or referral is delayed. Ministries of Health rely heavily on Community Health Volunteers, but CHVs often work far from facilities, under pressure, and with weak connectivity.

The second problem is visibility. A CHV may see the first signs of a cluster, but district teams often receive outbreak information through slow manual reporting. By the time the pattern is visible, response can already be late.

## The Goal

Build an AI-enabled prototype that simplifies symptom-based intake for CHVs, supports safe next steps, and turns field cases into real-time outbreak alerts for district officials.

## About Sentinel Health

Sentinel Health is a first-mile health intelligence tool for Africa. It starts with the CHV, because CHVs are already trusted by households and are often the first person a caregiver reaches before a clinic.

One intake creates two outcomes:

- The child gets faster triage support and clearer referral guidance.
- The district gets an earlier signal about where cases are rising.

The solution is designed for African health systems where distance, connectivity, workforce pressure, and delayed reporting are daily constraints. It does not replace CHVs or clinicians. It gives them a safer workflow, preserves human review, and helps Ministry teams see risk earlier.

## Where This Goes

The buildathon prototype can grow into a practical CHV platform:

- voice-assisted intake for low-literacy and high-pressure field settings
- Gemini-backed decision support for non-critical cases
- deterministic danger-sign escalation when urgent referral cannot wait on AI
- computer vision support for malnutrition screening
- offline-first sync for rural connectivity
- district dashboards for ward burden, syndrome trends, and outbreak alerts
- future DHIS2/HMIS integration for Ministry reporting

## The Tools

Google AI Studio, Gemini, Vertex AI, Vision API, Cloud Run, BigQuery.
